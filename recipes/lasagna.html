Lasagna

Description

A simple and truly satisfying lasagna recipie that some people say is the world's best.

Ingridents

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Sugar
  • Spices
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
